Grace Hui

I am a contemporary fiction author and occasional poet.

My work has been / will be published in: The Wave.

My current WIP is a contemporary fiction novel aiming to finish at 80,000 words. You can track my progress below.

March - May 2020

Outline and the first draft.

While balancing a full-time job, I outlined and wrote the first draft. I also conducted interviews with members of the Asian diaspora working in the main character's industry.

June - July 2020 (present)

The second draft.

This is when the magic happens and the novel takes shape. I am working on character arcs, relationship arcs, timelines, and everything imaginable to give flavour and texture to the book.

August - September 2020

Beta reading and feedback.

I will be looking for beta readers in August. If you're interested in having an exclusive first look, please fill out this quick form.

October 2020 onward

Querying!

I am an unagented author and will be seeking representation for this full-length contemporary fiction, which should finish at approximately 80,000 words. Please feel free to reach out to me by email, Twitter, or Facebook.
